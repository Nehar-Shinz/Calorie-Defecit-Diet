Day 1

Meal 1: Vegetable Upma

Portion: 1 cup

Calories: ~200 kcal

Nutrition: 5g protein, 6g fat, 32g carbs

Ingredients: Semolina (rava), onions, tomatoes, peas, carrots, mustard seeds, curry leaves.

Preparation: Toast semolina, sauté veggies, and spices, add water, and cook until soft.

Benefits: Light and filling with fiber from vegetables.

Meal 2: Paneer Bhurji with Roti

Portion: Paneer Bhurji (100g paneer) + 1 roti

Calories: ~300 kcal

Nutrition: 15g protein, 8g fat, 35g carbs

Ingredients: Crumbled paneer, onions, tomatoes, green chilies, turmeric, cumin, whole wheat roti.

Preparation: Sauté onions and tomatoes with spices, add paneer, and serve with roti.

Benefits: Protein-rich and satisfying vegetarian meal.

Day 2

Meal 1: Masala Oats

Portion: 1 cup cooked oats with vegetables

Calories: ~180 kcal

Nutrition: 7g protein, 5g fat, 25g carbs

Ingredients: Oats, onions, tomatoes, peas, carrots, cumin, coriander.

Preparation: Sauté the vegetables with your favorite spices. Add oats and water, and cook until the oats are tender and the liquid has been absorbed.

Benefits: High in fiber, filling, and quick.

Meal 2: Grilled Chicken Wrap / Paneer Wrap

Portion: 100g chicken or paneer + 1 whole wheat tortilla

Calories: ~350 kcal (chicken) / ~400 kcal (paneer)

Nutrition: 25g protein (chicken) / 15g protein (paneer), 8g fat, 30g carbs

Ingredients: Grilled chicken breast or paneer, whole wheat tortilla, lettuce, cucumber, onions, yogurt dressing.

Preparation: Grill the chicken or paneer, wrap in a tortilla with veggies and yogurt dressing.

Benefits: Balanced with lean protein and veggies.

Day 3

Meal 1: Lemon Rice

Portion: 1 cup cooked lemon rice

Calories: ~220 kcal

Nutrition: 4g protein, 6g fat, 40g carbs

Ingredients: Cooked rice, lemon juice, mustard seeds, turmeric, curry leaves, peanuts.

Preparation: Sauté mustard seeds, curry leaves, and peanuts in oil. Add turmeric, cooked rice, and lemon juice. Mix well and cook for a few minutes.

Benefits: Light, tangy, and easy to prepare.

Meal 2: Chicken Stir Fry / Paneer Stir Fry

Portion: 100g chicken or paneer + mixed vegetables

Calories: ~350 kcal (chicken) / ~400 kcal (paneer)

Nutrition: 25g protein (chicken) / 15g protein (paneer), 10g fat, 20g carbs

Ingredients: Chicken breast or paneer, bell peppers, onions, soy sauce, garlic, ginger.

Preparation: Stir-fry chicken or paneer with veggies, garlic, and ginger. Add soy sauce and cook until the chicken is done or the paneer is golden.

Benefits: High in protein and veggies, great for muscle recovery.

Calories: ~300 kcal

Day 6

Meal 1: Aloo Paratha

Portion: 1 paratha

Calories: ~250 kcal

Nutrition: 5g protein, 8g fat, 40g carbs

Ingredients: Whole wheat flour, boiled potatoes, cumin, coriander, green chilies.

Preparation: Knead whole wheat flour, stuff with spiced mashed potatoes, and roll. Cook with minimal oil.

Benefits: Comfort food with healthier preparation and controlled portion sizes.

Meal 2: Tandoori Chicken / Paneer with Salad

Portion: 150g chicken or 100g paneer + 1 cup salad

Calories: ~350 kcal (chicken) / ~400 kcal (paneer)

Nutrition: 30g protein (chicken) / 15g protein (paneer), 10g fat, 10g carbs

Ingredients: Chicken drumsticks or paneer, yogurt, tandoori masala, lemon, lettuce, cucumber, onions.

Preparation: Marinate chicken or paneer in yogurt and spices, grill or bake. Serve with salad.

Benefits: High-protein, low-carb meal suitable for both non-vegetarian and vegetarian diets.

Day 7

Calories: ~300 kcal (fish) / ~350 kcal (paneer)
